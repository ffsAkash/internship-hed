About the internship

Selected intern's day-to-day responsibilities include:

1. Customer engagement
2. Tele & showroom sales
3. MIS
4. Assist in analyzing and reviewing current business operations and workflows to identify inefficiencies, gaps, and opportunities for improvement.
5. Support data collection, reporting, and analysis to measure business effectiveness.
6. Collaborate with various departments (sales, customer care, franchise, and finance) to streamline operations.
7. Contribute to the development of SOPs, performance dashboards, and process checklists.
8. Assist in implementing CRM-based tracking mechanisms for real-time insights.
9. Conduct competitor analysis and benchmarking to enhance business strategies.
10. Participate in cross-functional projects to drive operational efficiency.

Digital Marketing Social Media Marketing

Who can apply

Only those candidates can apply who:

1. are available for full time (in-office) internship

2. can start the internship between 24th Nov'25 and 5th Jan'26

3. are available for duration of 3 months

4. have relevant skills and interests

* Women wanting to start/restart their career can also apply.

About Adamet Motors Private Limited

Website
At Adamet motors, we are committed to commencing a new wave in the mobility services of India. With our close understanding of the automobile market and transactional patterns, we aim to simplify the automotive experience for Indian customers. We believe in abiding by transparent business dealings with absolute integrity and honesty.

Adamet Motor Private Limited introduces autodealz.in as its first independent sub-brand. We are all set to proudly portray ourselves as one of the most trusted players in the pre-owned bike space. Owing to the current scenario, the pre-owned two-wheeler market lacks structure and confidence. It is always associated with ambiguity and a lack of trust among the customers. With our latest business model, we eliminate the mistrust and obscure part of the game. Absolutely legalized transactions with a thorough background check, we are here to offer our customers a whole new joyride.
